Nam Dinh Attractions

4 Best Places to Visit In Nam Dinh

Vieng Market

Lunar New Year perhaps consists of various traditions and festivals in Vietnam. Standing out from several spring activities is Vieng market, a unique market in Nam Dinh.

Thinh Long Beach

Thing Long is a new beach in Hai Hau, Nam Dinh that has only recently become popular.

Xuan Thuy National Park

Not only famous for spectacular religious architecture, Nam Dinh is a special destination for nature lovers with Xuan Thuy National Park, one of the country's biggest natural homes for hundreds of fauna species, especially birds.

Tran Temple

Tran Temple in Nam Dinh is a famous destination, where kings of the Tran Dynasty and their mandarins are worshiped.
